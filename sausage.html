Making a sausage

...

  1. Cut meat into chunks
  2. Freeze until nearly frozen
  3. Soak natural casings in warm water for 30 minutes, the rinse
  4. Grind the meat and fat
  5. Mix with spices and salt until the mixture is "tacky"
  6. Feed the meat mixture into a sausage stuffer or grinder attachment, filling the casings evenly without creating air pockets
  7. Twist the stuffed casings into desired lengths, alternating directions for each link to prevent unwinding
  8. Prick any remaining air bubbles with a pin and rest the sausages in the refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours or overnight to allow the casing to dry and flavors to set
