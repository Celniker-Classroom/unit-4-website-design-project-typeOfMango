Pineapple

Pineapple comes from South America and was introduced to Europe in the 1600s. They grow on shrubs and when multiple flowers from an unpollinated plant fuse, they form a fruit. Pineapple is often used on pizza, on a stick as a kabob, on yams with a cherry in the middle, as an addition to a fruit salad, burger, or Hawaiian pizza
